Privacy Policy for Email Statistics Extension

Effective Date: November 18, 2025

1. Introduction

This Chrome Extension ("Email Statistics Extension") is operated by MPM Projects Limited. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our extension.

2. Information We Collect

Our extension accesses the following information from your Gmail account:

  • Email metadata (sender, recipient, date, subject)
  • Email labels

We do NOT access or store the content/body of your emails.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information is used solely to:

  • Calculate and display email statistics
  • Show analytics about your email usage

All processing happens locally in your browser. We do NOT send your data to any external servers.

4. Data Storage

Your email data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do NOT store any of your Gmail data on our servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties.

6. Data Security

Your data remains on your local device and is protected by Chrome's security measures.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Revoke access to your Gmail at any time through Google account settings
  • Uninstall the extension to remove all stored data

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" above.
